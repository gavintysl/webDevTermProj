An endless card game of War! Play against the computer and reign supreme!

Game and term project created by Gavin Tysl





The game is simple. Draw one card and compare it against the computer's. Highest card wins, and winner takes both cards. Play until one person has all of the cards. In the event of a tie, THERE WILL BE WAR. Three cards will be drawn from each deck (if available) and the fourth card from each will be compared. Highest card wins. Winner takes all 8 cards.
